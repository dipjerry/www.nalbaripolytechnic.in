Polytechnic Course is a 3-year diploma based engineering course in India comes under Directorate of Technical Education. It is renowned as Diploma Engineering.

For getting admission on the basis of 10th, you must have to clear PAT (Polytechnic Admission Test). PAT is a Diploma Entrance Test which will make you eligible for taking admission in any Polytechnic college of Assam under Directorate of Technical Education. But, for appearing in the PAT you must fulfill its eligibility conditions.

Eligibility Conditions for PAT:

You must have completed your 10th from a recognized Education Board.
You must have completed your 10th with Mathematics and Science as compulsory subjects with 40% marks (in Average) in Mathematics and Science for General candidates 35% marks (in Average) in Mathematics and Science for SC candidates 33% marks (in Average) in Mathematics and Science for ST Candidates.
A candidate should be physically fit and mentally sound and should possess the minimum physical standard.
Candidates must be an Indian Citizen and Permanent resident of Assam.
