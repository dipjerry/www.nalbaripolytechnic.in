PRINTING

Department of Printing technology , established in the year 2017 for first time in North East India, is a crucial part of this institution .

It is an diploma Printing Technology course. Printing is a process for reproducing text and image, typically with ink on paper using a printing press. It is often carried out as a large-scale industrial process, and is an essential part of publishing and transaction printing. The course is solely dedicated towards the management of the jobs concerned with printing in a printing press. Diploma in Printing Technology is an diploma Printing Technology course.

Almost every procedures of assembling the films for the operating printing equipments and the well finished jobs of cutting the finished works; are enlightened to the students. Hence, altogether the course promises an individual, the quality and experiences that would further assist him to various job profiles. The duration of the course is three years. Candidates have many career options after having completed the course.
